The Cosmic Journey

In the distant future, humanity's thirst for exploration and knowledge drove us beyond the limits of Earth, deep into the boundless expanse of space. Among our greatest discoveries was the secret of harnessing the immense energy of stars, encoded within intricate cosmic constellations. Yet, the universe had its own enigmatic way of choosing which constellations revealed their secrets.

Thus, the game of Block Constellation was born—a cosmic ritual intertwining chance, strategy, and fate. Each block mined from the vast digital frontier of Bitcoin's blockchain forms a star, and every 144 blocks come together to shape a unique constellation. This celestial alignment, born from cryptographic patterns, reveals untold wealth and prestige.

Adventurers from across the galaxy gather, staking their sats upon these constellations, drawn by legends of immense rewards and the thrill of cosmic fortune. Each participant meticulously calculates their odds, aligning their strategy with celestial cycles, aiming to claim their share of the cosmic bounty.

As the 144th block completes the epoch, all eyes turn skyward, breathlessly awaiting the revelation. A hush sweeps over the digital cosmos as the winning constellation emerges from the intricate cryptographic formations of the blockchain.

Will you be among those whose destiny aligns with the stars, claiming your rightful reward, or will your wager become the foundation for another's fortune in the next cosmic cycle?

Welcome to Block Constellation—where blockchain creates the cosmos, and every epoch offers a new chance to align your fate with the stars.

How the Game Works

Choose Your Constellation

From 25 unique celestial patterns, select the constellations you believe will align with the cosmic energies of Bitcoin's blockchain.

Allocate Your Sats

Stake your bitcoin satoshis across your chosen constellations, creating your unique cosmic strategy.

Await The Revelation

Every 144 blocks (approximately 24 hours), a winning constellation is revealed by the cosmic forces of Bitcoin's blockchain.

Claim Your Rewards

If your chosen constellation aligns with the stars, claim your proportional share of the cosmic bounty.
